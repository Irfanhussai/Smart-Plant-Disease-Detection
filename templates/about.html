Why is it necessary to detect disease in plant ?

Since plant diseases can have a major effect on the development and production of the afflicted species, their detection is essential. Effective care requires early identification, even before outward symptoms manifest. This emphasizes how important it is to fill up research gaps in order to increase illness identification's accuracy and transparency. A plant pathologist's profession mostly involves diagnosis as it's critical to accurately identify the illness and its causative agent. Ineffective control methods might aggravate plant losses and waste time and money if they are not properly diagnosed. Consequently, fast and precise disease identification is essential to effective plant health management.

Prevent Plant Disease follow below steps:
  1. Implement Field Sanitation.
  2. Practice Crop Rotation.
  3. Inspect Plants for Diseases Before You Bring Them Home.
  4. Use Resistant Crop Varietie.
  5. Monitor Soil Health.
  6. Apply Biological Controls.
  7. Provide Good Air Circulation
  8. Remove Diseased Stems and Foliage
  9. Inspect crops for early signs of disease, pests, or nutrient deficiencies to implement corrective measures quickly.
